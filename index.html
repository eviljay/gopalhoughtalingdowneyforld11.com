MAKE SURE YOU HAVE A PLAN TO VOTE!

Voting by Mail

 If you're on the automatic vote-by-mail list, you may have already received your ballot.


Not sure about your status? Call the County Clerk's office at 848-863-4303.


If you'd like to request a vote-by-mail ballot, please visit www.monmouthcountyvotes.com.


IMPORTANT: The most efficient way to return your ballot is through a drop-box location. Find your nearest drop-box here: https://www.monmouthcountyvotes.com/voter-information/vote-by-mail-drop-box-locations/

Early In-Person Voting

Early in-person voting will run from Saturday, October 23 to Sunday, October 31.


You can find your early voting location and hours by visiting www.monmouthcountyvotes.com.

Election Day

Polls are open from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m. Find your polling location on the County Clerk's website: www.monmouthcountyvotes.com.
